Descriptions

Pasta is a versatile and beloved staple of Italian cuisine that has won hearts worldwide. It is made primarily from a simple dough of wheat flour and water, often enriched with eggs for added flavor and texture. The dough is shaped into various forms, ranging from long strands like spaghetti to intricate shapes like fusilli or stuffed varieties like ravioli. The beauty of pasta lies in its ability to adapt to countless culinary creations. It serves as the perfect canvas for a wide array of sauces, from rich and creamy Alfredo to robust and tangy marinara. It can be baked into comforting casseroles like lasagna or tossed in a light olive oil and garlic dressing for a fresh, minimalist meal.

Ingredients

  • Pasta (e.g., spaghetti, penne, fettuccine)
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes or tomato sauce
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil or parsley
  • Ground meat or vegetables (optional)
  • Heavy cream (optional for creamy sauces)
  • Butter (optional)
  • Chili flakes (optional for spice)

Steps

  1. Boil water in a large pot and add a generous amount of salt.
  2. Add pasta to the boiling water and cook according to the package instructions until al dente.
  3. Drain the pasta, reserving some of the cooking water, and set it aside.
  4. In a pan, heat olive oil over medium heat and saut√© garlic and onions until fragrant and translucent.
  5. Add tomatoes or tomato sauce, and let it simmer for a few minutes. Season with salt, black pepper, and optional spices like chili flakes.
  6. For creamy pasta, stir in heavy cream or a bit of butter to the sauce.
  7. Add cooked meat or vegetables, if desired, and let them cook in the sauce briefly.
  8. Toss the cooked pasta into the sauce, mixing well to coat evenly. If needed, add reserved pasta water to adjust the consistency.
  9. Sprinkle fresh herbs like basil or parsley, and optionally, grated Parmesan cheese on top.
  10. Serve hot and enjoy your delicious homemade pasta!
